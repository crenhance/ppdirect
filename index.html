PAVERS & DECK TILES FOR EXCEPTIONAL OUTDOOR SURFACES

Porcelain pavers are the newest addition to the hardscape industry innovation. With a variety of color options, sizes and functionality, Porcelain Pavers Direct inventory delivers pavers, interlocking deck tiles and pedestal support for homes and commercial properties alike. Their durability, longevity and resemblance to natural stone make these the elegant touch to create luxurious decks, patios, terraces, gardens and public plazas.

Elegant porcelain pavers are stylish and the easy-maintenance alternatives for either residential or commercial spaces. Optimal resistance to fire and frost, stains and scuffs.

Easy-to-install interlocking pavers – no nails, screws or adhesives needed – just snap them together to cover any exterior hard surfaces. Ideal for patios, pool decks, plazas and terraces.

Adjustable-height deck support pedestals produce perfectly level terraces, decks, plazas and patios, and support the installation of pavers over sloping or uneven surfaces.
